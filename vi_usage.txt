Here to put notes for using vi. 

:i to enter insert mode, which is very usefu.

ctrl+] to see a topic. 

ctrl+t to jump back to previous screen. 
ctrl+o 

in normal mode, $ to move cursor at the end of a line. 
And ^ to move the start. In contrast, in shell script, use ctrl+a or ctrl+e to do the same thing.
